Our HVAC Services

Comprehensive solutions for all your heating and cooling needs

HVAC Installation

Our expert technicians provide professional installation of all types of heating and cooling systems. We ensure proper sizing, placement, and configuration for optimal performance and efficiency.

Our Installation Services Include:

  • Central air conditioning systems
  • Furnaces and heat pumps
  • Ductless mini-split systems
  • Thermostat installation and programming
  • Air duct system design and installation
  • Zoning system implementation

HVAC Repair

When your heating or cooling system breaks down, you need fast and reliable repair services. Our technicians are trained to diagnose and fix issues with all makes and models of HVAC equipment.

Common Repair Services:

  • AC compressor replacement
  • Furnace ignition system repair
  • Refrigerant leak detection and repair
  • Electrical component replacement
  • Blower motor repair and replacement
  • Thermostat calibration and repair

Maintenance Contracts

Regular maintenance is key to extending the life of your HVAC system and preventing costly breakdowns. Our maintenance plans are designed to keep your system running efficiently year-round.

Maintenance Plan Benefits:

  • Priority scheduling for service calls
  • Discounts on repairs and parts
  • Two seasonal tune-ups per year
  • Comprehensive system inspection
  • Cleaning of essential components
  • Energy efficiency optimization

About ClimatePro

Your trusted partner for HVAC solutions since 2005

Our Story

Founded in 2005 by master HVAC technician John Williams, ClimatePro started as a small family business with a commitment to quality service and customer satisfaction. What began as a one-van operation has grown into one of the region's most trusted HVAC service providers.

Our Mission

At ClimatePro, our mission is to provide exceptional heating and cooling solutions that ensure our customers' comfort while promoting energy efficiency and environmental responsibility.

Our Values

We believe in honesty, integrity, and transparency in all our interactions. Our team is committed to continuous learning and staying updated with the latest HVAC technologies and best practices.
