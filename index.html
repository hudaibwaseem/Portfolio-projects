About Luxury Escape

Your trusted partner for premium travel experiences since 2005

Our Story

Founded in 2005 by travel enthusiasts Sarah and Michael Johnson, Luxury Escape began as a small boutique travel agency with a vision to create unforgettable luxury travel experiences. What started as a passion project has grown into one of the most respected luxury travel companies in the industry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to craft personalized, exceptional travel experiences that exceed our clients' expectations. We believe that travel should be transformative, inspiring, and seamless from start to finish.

Our Values

We are committed to excellence, authenticity, and sustainability in all our operations. We partner with eco-conscious luxury resorts and experiences that respect local cultures and environments.

Why Choose Us?

With over 18 years of experience, we have built relationships with the finest hotels, resorts, and experience providers around the world. Our team of travel experts personally vet every property and experience we offer.

Certifications

We are proud members of the Luxury Travel Network, ASTA (American Society of Travel Advisors), and have been awarded the Excellence in Travel award for five consecutive years.
